Hi, I'm Brenda Boudaie Zarrinkhoo, founder of Clinic Creative — a boutique
studio based in Los Angeles, building websites and branding exclusively
for medical and dental practices.

### Why healthcare, specifically

Most agencies try to be everything to everyone. I chose the opposite:
work with one kind of client, and understand that client's world better
than anyone else. Healthcare websites have to do more than look
good — they need to build trust with patients, meet the standards of a
regulated industry, and make it effortless for someone to book an
appointment. That's a different job than building a website for a
restaurant or a retail brand, and it deserves a specialist.

### How I work

Since 2019, I've partnered with independent physicians, specialists, and
dental practices throughout every stage of growth — from a brand-new
practice's first website to a modernization of an existing one. Every
project is hands-on: you work directly with me, not an account manager,
from discovery through launch and beyond.
